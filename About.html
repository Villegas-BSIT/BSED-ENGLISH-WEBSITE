About Our Institution

Davao Vision Colleges, Inc.

A Brief History

Year 2007, Rev. Dr. Kim Won Sun, the senior pastor and chairman of the board of Yeomchang Central Presbyterian Church in Seoul, South Korea had a vision to start a non-sectarian tertiary institution in the Philippines.. Leadership and missionary training are the primary goals of the institution which is situated in the three hectares land in Stone Rock Village, Catalunan Gande, Davao City. In the year 2008, the constructions of buildings and preparation of SEC, CHED and Government-related documents were made. Rev. Iluminado Macahig Jr,, Rev. Fidel Japos, Ph.D., Rev. Abdon C. Canonoy, Jr., Ph.D., Rosita J. Bustamante, Ph.D., and Prof. Rev. Jose Victolero were chosen to serve as the College Board of Trustees. Through collaborative efforts of the Board, the SEC Registration was given on April 30, 2008 and permits to operate Bachelor of Arts in Theology, BSBA, BEED, BSED and BSIT were given by CHED RO XI on February 2009. The Philippine Immigration had approved and gave permit to accept foreign students in the year 2010. The college has given a Government Recognition to operate 5 courses in the year 2012.
